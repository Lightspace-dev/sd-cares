What is San Diego Cares ?

On Saturday, December 16, 2017, San Diego Cares: It’s in our Blood will launch at the Town and Country Convention Center as our city’s new annual blood drive.

San Diego Cares will celebrate the spirit of San Diegans who care deeply for our community at a time of high-need for hospital patients. Join local sports teams and legends in giving back. San Diego Cares will provide San Diego families and individuals with a meaningful new holiday tradition: giving the gift of life-saving blood to those in need in our community. Businesses, nonprofits, schools, churches and community groups can support the event as sponsors and participants.

Helping those in need

Why should I get involved?

By participating in San Diego Cares, you will help ensure that life-saving blood is available for your family, friends, and neighbors when they need it.

The San Diego Blood Bank serves our community and saves lives. But this great work can only happen with the help of people like you.

The holiday season is a critical time for the Blood Bank, so your involvement will make a significant difference for those in need.
